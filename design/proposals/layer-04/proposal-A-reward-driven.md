# Layer 4 — Acquisition Model · Proposal A · REWARD-DRIVEN

**"The pack fills at the gate."** · pipeline-test-02 · ROUNDELAY

**Thesis (committed).** New content enters a run **mainly as the world's gladness**: the deck grows, the materials arrive, and the purse fills **because askings were answered.** The single richest flow — canon's own `the-glad-load` — *is* the acquisition engine, paying a **taught journey-piece** (chosen from a few, arriving with the town's name + grain), a **material introduction** (an introduction-jar that skips the courtship), **bright handsels**, and **map-intel chalked outward**, all in one bundle scaled off the gatepost spiral (**rings-in = load-out**). Bigger, later contracts pay **richer choices**, not merely more. Around this spine sit two secondary reward-nodes canon already hangs — **hearth-right rest-nodes** (tell the year → *deepen* a card) and **festival / Round-Fair wins** (draft, teach, and the doubled fair-answer glad-load) — and the leanest possible supporting faucets (setting-out first-gift, waymeet swaps, the whittling floor). Card thinning is by **`retire`/last-lighting**, waymeet gifting, and the **Quiet Walk** reset. Supply richness is gated **upward** by the season/Paling (rings rise → loads rise) and by gleam (Filter-2 → which askings, hence which loads, are offerable) — so acquisition rises *with* the crown it must eventually stand.

Everything below traces to a WORLD.md / ledger id, mints no new currency (acquisition adds R5 pieces, R6 materials, R2 handsels only — never a fifth token), and is expressible as data over the L3 primitives. **No generic card-shop:** there is no vendor of cards anywhere in Roundelay; you are *given* pieces by delighted towns, taught them by masters, or you court the stuff and bench them yourself.

---

## 0. The acquisition square — what can enter a run, and by what door

Only **three** things are ever *newly acquired* into a run (the L2 substrate + strategic currency). The other three L2 resources are flows you earn or route, never "acquire content into":

| Enters the run | L2 id | The one door it comes through (this layer) | Never entered by |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Journey-pieces** (the deck) | R5 | **taught at answered askings** (the-glad-load) · fair teach/draft · first-gift · waymeet swap · walking-with-masters · **benched from courted stock** | a card-shop (there is none) |
| **Courted stock** (materials) | R6 | **introduction-jars from the-glad-load** · the two-gate courting · apprentice floor · festival-glass at fairs · twice-benched from `retire` · last-red from gleaning | a coin purchase of proud stock |
| **Handsels** (money) | R2 | **bright handsels from the-glad-load** · whittling the apprentice floor · the-shavings-share · glad-price brightened change | debt / wage / fee |

**Standing (R3)**, **attention (R1)**, and **the Paling (R4)** are *earned, spent, or routed* — never "acquired as content." They are the **gates and clocks** that decide how much of the three above enters, and when (§4). This keeps the acquisition model inside the L2 lean set: **acquisition is a bundle paid across existing sub-economies, not a new economy.**

**The one-sentence mental model (intuitiveness gate):** *answer the town, and the town fills your pack — a piece, a jar, a purse, a road ahead — and the gladder the need, the fuller the load.* Every mechanism below is legible from that sentence plus its name.

## 1. The full acquisition model — every source

### 1A · New journey-pieces (R5) — the deck grows

**S-CARD-1 · The taught piece from the-glad-load — THE SPINE (primary, ~55–65% of gross card influx).**
Answer a town's asking (L1 §3, the working) and, at the gate, the town **teaches one journey-piece** — *"a journey-piece taught by whoever the town loves best"* (`the-glad-load`). Mechanically:
- **A choose-from-a-few draft.** The town lays out a small hand of pieces it could teach (2 in the Green Going → up to 4–5 after a great asking); you keep **one**. This is *reward as a decision*, not a handout.
- **Arrives with the town's name + grain.** The kept piece carries its giver's name and craft-suit (`the-giving-line` / `deck-autobiography`); its grain is drawn from **what your gleam is grained toward** (`grain-tagged-standing`) crossed with the town's own craft — so *the country teaches you toward the maker you are being*, with no class menu.
- **Tier scales off the spiral: rings-in = load-out.** A three-ring town teaches a modest piece; a five-ring "poem" offers a **richer choice** (more options, higher mark/ceiling/effect-density); *"what follows a great asking is less a load than a season"* — the crown's own teaching (§1A-S6).
- **Trace:** `contract-payoff-bundle`/`the-glad-load`; `the-giving-line`/`deck-autobiography`; `escalating-contracts`; `grain-tagged-standing`. As data: the taught piece is a card (L3 §0) whose effect-density rises with tier.

**S-CARD-2 · Benched from courted stock (secondary, the maker's own hands).**
Courted materials (R6) are **worked into pieces at the bench** — a *between-turn* action (L3 §7: `bench` is off-turn, not a primitive). This is the reward-driven model's *self-authored* card source: gleam vouches the stuff, you perform its term (the `court` primitive), and off-turn you bench it into a new piece grained to the material. It is subordinate to teaching — it costs a courtship and a bench-night — and it is how a **Mannerly** build turns proud stock into a deck. Trace: `journey-pieces-deck`; `two-gate-courting`; `graded-material-market`.

**S-CARD-3 · Fair teach & draft (secondary, windowed).**
At a **Round-Fair intercept** (`fair-hub-intercept`, the one moving node) the fair offers draft/trade/**teach**, and *"retells everything at twice the size"* — a fair-taught piece is the **richest single teach** in the game, and festival-glass-grained pieces exist only here (`festival-glass`). Windowed: the Fair is *"a week ahead of autumn"* and passes each country once, so this is a **routing prize, not a farm** (§4, §5). Trace: `fair-hub-intercept`/`the-round-fair`.

**S-CARD-4 · The fair-answer contest (secondary, high-variance).**
A **rival-maker node** (`fair-answer-rival-contest`): you answer an asking alongside an NPC way, attention the contested pool. **Win → the full reward and the telling** (a *doubled* glad-load, hence the fair's richest taught choice); **lose → the smaller gift** and lost tempo (never Standing directly). The reach-for-more of the acquisition layer. Trace: `fair-answer-rival-contest`; `overkill-to-standing`.

**S-CARD-5 · Walking-with-masters / season-shadowing (secondary, archetype door).**
Map events where you **adopt a master's constraint for a segment and exit with an off-way card carrying their name** (`walking-ways-cardpools`). This is the archetype-selection door and a *focused* card source (grain bonuses reward focus). Subordinated here to the glad-load spine: it seeds identity, the glad-load feeds the volume. Trace: `walking-ways-cardpools`.

**S-CARD-6 · The setting-out first-gift (once, the loadout).**
The run opens with **one journey-piece taught at the gate** by "someone's grandmother," plus mostly-dull handsels and a first road-mark (`the-wander-year` setting-out). This *is* the diegetic loadout: what you make in the first-gift ceremony seeds starting grain + way-affinity + the first lantern reading. One-time. Trace: `wander-year-run-frame`.

**S-CARD-7 · Waymeet-table swap (secondary, luck-of-the-road).**
At circuit crossings, **take a storied piece off the plank table, leave one you won't keep bright** (`the-waymeet-table`). A taken piece *"arrives already storied,"* a **head-start on waking**. This is *acquisition-as-gifting* — the road's answer to a shop, with **no shopkeeper and no accounts** — and its inventory is only what prior walkers (and your past runs) chose to leave, so it is thin and unfarmable. Trace: `the-waymeet-table`.

**S-CARD-8 · Red-thread-days at the crown (once/late, unlocks).**
At the year-end **combing/crown**, the gathered ways offer legible boss-modifiers, one of which is **courted introductions that unlock proud materials** (→ benched into crown-scale pieces) (`red-thread-days-boss-modifiers`). A capstone supply event, not a loop. Trace: `red-thread-days-boss-modifiers`.

**Card upgrades (deepen, don't add) — the hearth path.**
Distinct from the sources above, **hearth-right rest-nodes upgrade the deck without growing it.** At a fire you **tell one event from this run's history** (selection, never a cost — `hearth-right-rest-telling`); *"the house wakes around it, that event's tag becoming the night's boon."* The boon is spent to **deepen one held piece**: `warm` (+printed delight), `keep` (one extra season of freshness), or an eased first-waking next dawn (the piece begins tomorrow already part-`rest`-ed toward its fixed mark — *never lowering the mark*, L3-legal). This is the "tell a story → upgrade a card" node: **reward as narrative, upgrade as gift.** Twice-benched stock (from `retire`, below) does the same for *benched* pieces — nine-tenths of the waking already fired into the grain. Trace: `hearth-right-rest-telling`; `porch-meta-home`; `the-twice-benched`.

### 1B · New materials (R6) — the pack's garden is planted

**S-MAT-1 · Introduction-jar from the-glad-load — THE REWARD PATH (primary).**
Every answered asking loads *"an introduction-jar of the local proud stuff already courted"* (`the-glad-load`) — a **banked courtship collapsed into a gift**, among the most valuable things a pack can hold because it *"collapses weeks of courtship into a gift"* (`introduction-jars-freshness`). This is how answering askings *directly* stocks the proud-material shelf **without spending gleam or coin** — the reward-driven material spine. Trace: `the-glad-load`; `introduction-jars-freshness`.

**S-MAT-2 · The two-gate courting (secondary, the standing engine).**
The canon acquisition path for proud stock: **the vouching** (a *gleam gate* — no coin) then **the terms** (a performed micro-condition — the `court` primitive) (`two-gate-courting`). Gleam gates *which* stuff will state terms this run (market widens with Standing); grain decides *which* materials answer (`grain-tagged-standing`). Trace: `two-gate-courting`; `graded-material-market`.

**S-MAT-3 · Apprentice-stuffs (the floor, zero-gate).**
Straw, clay, withy, tallow — **courtable at every node by any hands**, the comeback path and the daily bread-work, *and the raw stock of handsel-whittling* (`the-apprentice-stuffs`). Always available; wakes thin, pales fastest — a doorway, never a destination. Trace: `apprentice-floor-materials`.

**S-MAT-4 · Festival-glass at fairs / high-attention nodes (venue-bound).**
Acquirable **only where crowds gather** (`festival-glass`); grants passive attention when benched. The Fairwright's material; a routing prize. Trace: `festival-glass`; `graded-material-market`.

**S-MAT-5 · Twice-benched from `retire` (thinning's yield).**
A chosen **last-lighting** returns twice-benched grain that **wakes at ~nine-tenths the attention of fresh stock** (`the-twice-benched`). The Evener's fuel; a sink made visibly generative. Keys **only to chosen last-lightings, never failure states** (anti-salvage, binding). Trace: `the-twice-benched`; `evener-sacrifice-velocity`.

**S-MAT-6 · Last-red from gleaning (pale-route harvest).**
Gleaning the **left-behind** (never the lived-in) yields last-red scaling with paleness (`pale-route-harvest`/`last-red`), spent only as a catalyst on **woken re-makings** (the `soothe` primitive). A board-pressure lever, capped 1 node / 1 knack / 1 season. Trace: `pale-route-harvest`; `red-thread-marker`.

**S-MAT-7 · Waymeet-table jars (luck-of-the-road).** As S-CARD-7, for jars/stock. Thin, unfarmable. Trace: `the-waymeet-table`.

### 1C · New handsels (R2) — the purse fills

**S-COIN-1 · Bright handsels from the-glad-load (primary reward path).**
*"Handsels rung bright that very evening by the whole square's glad palms"* (`the-glad-load`) — the single richest coin inflow, scaled to the spiral. Trace: `the-glad-load`; `handsel-currency-decay`.

**S-COIN-2 · Whittling the apprentice floor (the faucet — anyone can make money).**
Handsels are **whittled from apprentice-stuffs** (`apprentice-floor-materials`): the always-available money faucet, the Quiet Walker's way back. Near-dull until circulated. Trace: `apprentice-floor-materials`; `handsel-currency-decay`.

**S-COIN-3 · The-shavings-share (the bench byproduct — the `whittle` primitive).**
Big attended plays throw off **the-shavings-share** — a *bound sub-portion of the bench RETURN* carved by the telling (L2 §4, mint-clean; **competes with the local-table dawn**, mints nothing) into a handsel-already-begun. The bench→money coupling. Trace: `bright-shavings-byproduct`/`the-shavings-share`; `whittle` (L3).

**S-COIN-4 · Glad-price brightened change (circulation, bounded).**
Glad spending returns change *"a shade brighter"* (`the-glad-price`) — value from circulation, **bounded below its tempo/attention cost** (no money-printer; L2 §5, binding). Trace: `glad-price-read`.

**S-COIN-5 · Doorstep & waymeet small change (income valves).**
Doorstep askings pay *"a handsel or two"* (`doorstep-micro-contracts`); the waymeet table holds quiet handsels left to stay lit. Sparse-by-law low-room valves. Trace: `doorstep-micro-contracts`; `the-waymeet-table`.

### 1D · Map-intel (information) — the road ahead is acquired too

Answering an asking **chalks road-marks a day's walk ahead** (`the-glad-load`: *"road-marks chalked outward … word, never a telling"*), and the shared **map-intel** economy means your **past runs' marks persist** (`map-intel-preview`). Acquisition of *knowledge* — spiral counts, asking-tier silhouettes, dawn-richness — rides the same reward bundle. Aims eyes, moves no reading (courier law). Trace: `map-intel-preview`; `the-glad-load`.

## 2. The acquisition arc across the wander-year

The arc is authored by the reward spine itself: **askings start kettle-sized and rare in a thin spring, thicken and richen through summer, and pay seasons-worth by autumn** — so the deck is *pulled* up the exact curve the rising crown demands. Numbers are napkin estimates for the executable check (§5); game-loop tunes them.

| Season / leg | Askings answered (est.) | Taught choice offered | Deck (start→end) | Materials | Purse | The feel |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| **Green Going** (spring) | 3–4 (kettle) | choose-1-of-2, apprentice-grain | **7 → ~9** | apprentice floor; first jars | mostly-dull; whittling | *the shy market* — thin loads, learn the valleys (`the-glad-load`, `the-apprentice-stuffs`) |
| **Long Light** (high summer) | 5–6 | 1-of-3; first proud-grain teaches | **~9 → ~14** | jars flow; first courting; fair glass | chiming; shavings-jar glows | *the engine turns* — glad-loads arrive, the Fair crosses (`fair-hub-intercept`) |
| **Deep Gold** (late summer) | 4–5 (bigger) | 1-of-4; five-ring poems | **~14 → ~18** | twice-benched from first `retire`; proud shelf | singing; glad-loads thicken | *abundance* — the first enormous dawns, richer choices |
| **Red Walk** (autumn) | 3–4 (great) + crown | 1-of-4/5; crown teaches a season | **~18 → ~20**, thinned lean | crown introductions; festival-glass | rich mid-air, cannot sit | *stand the crown* — the deck the year built, at boss scale |
| **Wintering** | — (the telling) | — | persists 1 bead → meta | — | — | the year handed on mid-song |

**Why on-curve, not runaway (the load-bearing claim):** gross card influx ≈ **1 taught piece per answered town asking** (§1A-S1), and askings are **rate-limited three ways** — worked-mornings on the calendar (a finite year), lantern presence (only towns hang lanterns), and gleam (Filter-2 decides which loads are offerable). So the deck cannot outrun the year: **~20 answered askings** across a run, minus thinning (§3) and the choose-**one**-keep-one draft, lands an end-deck of **~18–22 pieces** — grown enough that the whole engine can stand the calendar-floored crown, slow enough that spring is genuinely thin. Early rarity is *authored by the weather* (kettle askings, small choices), late power is *authored by the same weather* (poems and the crown teach richest) — **the reward curve and the escalation curve are the same curve** (L1 §5).

## 3. Card thinning & removal — keeping the deck lean

Acquisition without removal is bloat; the reward model pairs every faucet with a canon drain:

- **`retire` / last-lighting (the primary thinner, the Evener line).** Perform an inert/held piece **to destruction**: permanent thin **+** its worth RETURNs to *this node's* local table (richer dawn) or the room now, **+** it yields **twice-benched** stock (S-MAT-5). *"Sinks made visibly generative"* — thinning *is* an acquisition of material. Keys only to **chosen** last-lightings, never failure. Trace: `evener-sacrifice-velocity`; `the-twice-benched`; `retire` (L3).
- **Waymeet-table gifting (S-CARD-7 in reverse).** **Leave** a piece you won't keep bright; it walks the road storied, its small blessing following you a leg (`the-waymeet-table`). Thinning-as-gift, no accounts.
- **Perform-to-destruction / exhaust.** Pieces *"given away or performed to destruction"* (`journey-pieces-deck`); the Untold's one-shots exhaust and never repeat (`untold-ratchet-tempo`). Tempo decks self-thin by design.
- **Material freshness reversion (garden, not hoard).** Untended courted stock **reverts to ordinary** within a season (`introduction-jars-freshness`) — a self-thinning that keeps the pack *"a garden, not a hoard."* Not the deck, but the acquisition surface that feeds it.
- **The Quiet Walk (the run-scale reset).** Standing-zero ends the run; the deck is not carried forward — *"every re-spark is a fresh maker, walking a fresh verse"* (I-013, the fresh verse). Exactly **one woken work persists** to the meta-layer (`quiet-walk-runend-meta`); the winter-telling likewise hands on **one bead** (`winter-telling-victory`). Between-run inflation is impossible by canon: the meta is **gifts-only**, sized to the tier reached (L1 §6). Trace: `quiet-walk-runend-meta`; `winter-telling-victory`.

**Net thinning ≈ 6–10 pieces/run** (retires + gifts + exhausts), which is what holds the ~20-gross influx to an ~18–22 end-deck and lets aggressive archetypes (Kilnfast's *"smallest, densest deck,"* the Untold's thin fast deck) run *below* that.

## 4. How the season/Paling and gleam gate supply

Supply is not flat — it is **gated upward** so it rises with the crown:

**The Paling / season gates (board-side — richer *and* harder together):**
- **Rings-in = load-out.** The spiral count sets *both* the asking's difficulty *and* the glad-load's richness (`paling-arithmetic-compounding` → `contract-payoff-bundle`). A skipped three-ring town becomes a five-ring poem: a **richer taught choice + a half-cart load**, but a harder need. The *same number* raises reward and difficulty — the loop's central risk dial, and the reason richer acquisition is never free.
- **The weather floor.** The seasonal seep raises the **minimum** need with the year (`escalation-is-the-weather`), so late loads are inherently richer *and* stand on bigger needs — even for a turtle.
- **Venue gating.** Festival-glass (S-MAT-4) and fair teaches (S-CARD-3) exist **only at crowd nodes**, on the Fair's *"week ahead of autumn"* window — richest supply is **routed to**, never farmed.
- **Pale-route yield.** Last-red / twice-benched scale with paleness and retirement — supply richest exactly where pressure is highest (`pale-route-harvest`).

**The gleam gates (maker-side — which supply is offerable at all):**
- **Filter-2 decides which askings light tonight** (`two-filter-lantern-sizing`) — hence **which glad-loads are reachable**. A dimming maker watches offers (and their loads) descend the ladder; a bright maker unlocks the richest teaches. Supply of *the reward spine itself* is gleam-gated.
- **Grain decides which grain the country teaches and which stock answers** (`grain-tagged-standing`) — acquisition has **build identity**, not volume-only.
- **Market width for courting opens with gleam** (`two-gate-courting`) — proud-stock supply widens as you rise.
- **Gleam is a gate, never spent** — no acquisition *costs* Standing; courting *"a key that turns, never a coin that leaves."* Reward-driven acquisition never drains the fail-resource.

**Net:** the richest supply (crown-scale teaches, proud stock, doubled fair loads) is **unlocked by being bright and by routing the pale country late** — precisely the conditions under which you must stand the rising crown. Supply and demand climb the same weather.

## 5. The anti-degenerate story (`napkin_flags` claimed: 0)

An **executable** model: acquisition grows the deck enough to stand the rising crown, but not trivially fast; no single source dominates; no degenerate/infinite loop.

**(a) No single source dominates — even with the-glad-load as the spine.**
The spine is *primary*, not *sole*, and is **structurally rate-limited**, so it cannot become a runaway:
- **Cards** ≈ 55–65% glad-load teach, the rest across bench-craft, fair, first-gift, waymeet, walking-with-masters — **≥3 independent doors** for the deck.
- **Materials** ≈ split glad-load jars / courting / apprentice-floor / gleaning / twice-benched — **no one door > ~40%**.
- **Handsels** ≈ split glad-load / whittling / shavings-share / glad-price — a **closed, mint-clean** round (L2 §5).
- The glad-load's own throughput is **capped by answered-asking count**, which is capped by **worked mornings (the finite calendar) × lantern presence × gleam-gated offers**. You cannot answer more askings than the year holds, and each node answers **once** (it re-colors, the spiral resets). **The spine is bounded by the clock, not exploitable.**

**(b) On-curve clearable — the deck grows *only by* engaging the escalation.**
Because **rings-in = load-out**, richer acquisition *always* arrives on a harder need under attention + `the-reach` risk (L1 §5). A static/turtle engine answers few askings → thin loads → an un-standable crown; a reaching engine grows its deck on schedule *and* risks Standing to do it. Growth is gated behind the win-condition, not handed out — the turtle-dodge is closed by **the crown being un-standable without a grown deck**, exactly as L1 §5/§6 require.

**(c) No degenerate / infinite loop — each carried risk discharged.**
- **Infinite-card generation — CLOSED.** No node re-issues an answered asking; the glad-load fires once per node, per year. No `draw`/`retire` loop mints pieces (they move/thin the pack, never create from nothing). Bench-craft consumes a courted material per piece (freshness-limited). **No card faucet runs without a finite input.**
- **Infinite-handsel generation — CLOSED.** Whittling consumes apprentice-stuffs (a courting each); the-shavings-share is **carved from the bench RETURN and competes with the dawn** (mint-clean, L2 §4/§5c); glad-price brightening is **bounded below its tempo/attention cost** (binding); idle handsels **lapse to the dawn**. *The money's only sink is the world's only income* — trading nets zero regard.
- **Skip-to-farm-the-glad-load — CLOSED.** Skipping compounds the spiral → a bigger *future* load, but the asking is proportionally harder, the board pressure (`combing`) rises toward a great asking, and **peak-gleam sizing, not paling-farming, carries the pressure curve** (binding, L1 §5/§8; anti-paling-tool ceilings from L4 hooks 3–4 hold: red thread 1/1/1, last-red woken-re-makings-only, gleaning never on inhabited/answered nodes). You still answer each rich town **once**.
- **Waymeet / fair farming — CLOSED.** Waymeet inventory is only what walkers left (thin, no accounts); fair teaches are windowed to the Fair's single passage. Neither is repeatable on demand.
- **Deliberate-failure farming — CLOSED.** Spilling yields `lane-glimmers` worth **< one Standing point**, claimable **only by later visitors, never the spiller** — EV-negative, so no one flops to acquire (`spill-boons`).
- **Twice-benched salvage — CLOSED.** Keys **only to chosen last-lightings, never failure states** (anti-salvage, binding).
- **Between-run inflation — CLOSED.** Fresh-verse clamp: no deck carries over; meta is **gifts-only, one bead, sized to tier** — no min-engagement farm (L1 §6; I-013).
- **Mint-ban backstop.** No acquisition flow creates or destroys regard — every windfall (a glad-load) is the town's *own* overflow of delight at a real re-making, never minted on top. With no net-creation, no loop runs positive indefinitely.

**`napkin_flags` claimed: 0.** No dominant source, on-curve-clearable (grows only by engaging escalation), no infinite/degenerate loop. Carried items reduce to **game-loop numeric bounds** (taught-tier curve, thinning rate, glad-price coefficient), not structural holes.

## 6. Consistency with L1 loop / L2 resources / L3 primitives

**L1 (core loop) — affirmed.** Acquisition *is* `the-glad-load` (L1 §2.6, §3), fired at **fulfilment only**, scaled off the gatepost spiral. The taught piece **arrives with the town's name** (`the-giving-line`); an acquired piece is still **inert until woken by a play** (`play-is-the-only-gate`, `fired-vs-held`) — acquisition doesn't bypass the exchange gate. Calendar-gating rides **worked mornings**, not travel (L1 §1). Hearth upgrades are **selection, never cost** (`hearth-right`). Nothing here touches the Standing meter except through canon-legal doors (overflow, the spilling).

**L2 (resources) — affirmed.** Acquisition adds only **R5 / R6 / R2** — **no fifth token**; the-glad-load pays *across* the existing sub-economies (System D → B, C, A) exactly as L2 §2 maps it. Materials stay **one graded R6 class**, courted (gleam-gated, never purchased) or gifted as jars — handsels **never buy proud stock** (L2 §8 P1). The-shavings-share is the **bound RETURN sub-portion** (mint-clean). The Handsel Round stays **closed**. Every source respects the grammar of light and the mint ban.

**L3 (primitives) — affirmed.** Taught/benched/gifted pieces are **cards = data over the 14 primitives** (L3 §0); no new primitive is needed. Acquisition and thinning ride existing verbs: **`draw`** (fair/first-gift search), **`retire`** (last-lighting → twice-benched + local-table), **`court`** (two-gate stock into the pack), **`whittle`** (the-shavings-share → handsels), **`soothe`** (last-red on re-makings), and **`warm`/`keep`** (the hearth-right upgrade — *raising delight/freshness, never lowering a fixed mark*). The between-turn **bench** (material→piece) is off-turn by design (L3 §7), so card-crafting adds no in-play primitive. Grammar-of-light and the play-gate hold at every acquisition edge.

**D-002 / D-003 — affirmed.** Every source is the world's own gladness worn as physics — a town loading a pack past sensible fullness at a dawn gate, a grandmother teaching at the setting-out, a plank table at a crossroads, a fire where the year is told. **No card-shop, no debt, no wage, no court, no paperwork.** Each source paints (§1 visuals in WORLD.md). Wonder, not a store.
