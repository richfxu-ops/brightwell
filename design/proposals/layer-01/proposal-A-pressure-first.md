# Layer 1 — Core Loop (core_loop_pressure) · Proposal A — PRESSURE-FIRST

**Working title of the loop:** *The Wander-Year Working* — one maker, one verse of a Round, walked from spring into the year's own out-breath.

**Framing commitment (A, pressure-first):** the loop is derived from the run's arc and the single tension that arc is built to squeeze — *need aiming at your brightness, against a light that thins when work disappoints.* Escalating askings and depletable gleam are not two systems bolted to a deckbuilder; they are one law of attention read from two sides (WORLD.md L1, "the world's need aiming itself at your brightness; the world's trust gathered on your skin"). The moment-to-moment play loop falls out of that pressure as its consequence, not the reverse. Everything below concretizes the **D-001** spine (roguelike economy deckbuilder · escalating contracts · a depletable Standing fail-resource) into a playable loop, and traces every mechanic to a WORLD.md element and ledger id. No mechanic is invented that the canon did not already imply.

**The one-line spine of the loop:** *You spend the year performing needs into being re-made; each performance can overflow into Standing or fall short into dimming; the size of what you are asked to make ratchets up with the brightest you have been and with every need you walk past; the year ends when your light does — or, still lit, when you stand the crown the year's own arithmetic built for you.*

---

## 0. The three laws the whole loop obeys (binding, from canon)

These are not loop mechanics; they are the physics every loop mechanic must not violate. Stated once, up front, because the gate for L1 is intuitiveness — a player must be able to predict any mechanic from these.

- **The grammar of light (L1, binding):** *places pale, people gleam, things wake.* Three channels, never crossing. → The Paling only ever touches **board-state** (nodes, resources, routes). Standing (gleam) is a key-light on the **maker only**. Waking is one gold edge on a **journey-piece only**. No mechanic may move a loss from one channel into another.
- **The three settlings + the one exchange gate (`the-three-settlings` / `play-as-exchange-gate`):** the loop's three readings — **node color (paleness), Standing (gleam), card wakefulness** — *never convert directly into one another.* Every transfer between them must pass through **a played journey-piece.** The act of play is the world's only exchange gate. This is why the game is a deckbuilder at all: cards are not a UI for the economy, they are the *only door* between its rooms.
- **Conservation — nothing is noticed away (`the-round-of-light` / attention-conservation):** attention spent always visibly arrives somewhere (into a piece as waking, to the maker as Standing, into a node as re-coloring); unspent attention is never destroyed, only **returned** — end-of-turn surplus seeps to the local table and comes back in tomorrow's dawn. There is no discard-to-void anywhere in the loop; a "where it went" readout replaces it.

## 1. The run and round/turn structure — the wander-year as pressure clock

Traces: `the-wander-year` / **wander-year-run-frame**; `the-four-lights`; `the-inked-round` / **run-map-circuit**.

**A run is one wander-year.** Fixed length, set by a seasonal **calendar track**: out on the morning after the spring even-morrow, home (one way or the other) by the **first still dawn** of winter. The calendar is the run's master resource — you cannot buy more of it, and it is the same clock as the escalation curve.

**The year has four walked legs, then the unwalked wintering** (`the-four-lights`). The critical, load-bearing fact: **the seasonal seep-rate curve *is* the escalation clock.** Canon holds the seep near-still in deep winter, waking gently through spring, blowing strongest in late summer and autumn — so a maker who sets out in spring walks *into* the out-breath, and the pressure rises on its own, not by any designer's hand:

| Leg | Season | Seep / pressure | What the loop feels like |
|---|---|---|---|
| **The Green Going** | spring | near-still, waking | kettle-sized askings, thin ordinary dawns, spirals turning slowly; the shy market — learn what this year's valleys answer to |
| **The Long Light** | high summer | gentle→strong | the biggest working *days*, thick crowds, everything states its terms; the mending question at every gate — answer now or let it ripen |
| **The Deep Gold** | late summer | strong | spirals turning visibly, gleaning country opening, the first enormous dawns; the engine turns |
| **The Red Walk** | autumn | strongest | the seep at full, the Fair a week ahead of the front, the countryside's missing gathering toward seven — the leg every other leg aimed at |
| **The Wintering** | winter | near-still | unwalked; the telling, or the road's memorial |

Escalating contracts are therefore *the year getting older around you* — non-negotiable, no one's fault, and felt as weather rather than a difficulty slider.

**A turn is one worked morning** (`the-wander-year`, "a turn is one morning wide"). Structure of every turn, exactly as canon lays it: **one dawn, one road, one fire.**

1. **Dawn (the morning) — income.** The day's regard is issued. You gain an attention pool: a flat universal base **+ a draw from the local table** (paler node = richer dawn), **+ yesterday's returned surplus** (conservation). You draw a hand of journey-pieces from your pack.
2. **The work — the performance** (see §2). You stand in the morning and *do the day's one thing*: answer an accepted asking, court a material, scout a crown, or claim a hearth. The performance is the deckbuilder encounter.
3. **The fire — the close.** The turn ends at a claimed hearth / joined porch / made camp. The day's **telling** is given or kept (a selection, never a cost — `hearth-right`). Attention surplus seeps to the table for tomorrow; in-room Standing lands now, distant Standing walks the road as a telling (see §4).

**A leg is the road between two high days** (equinoxes, the Long Day, first-frost fires, the Fair's passage). Turn-count-per-leg is a free tuning parameter and *not* fiction: **only worked mornings are turns** — the road's un-worked miles fold politely into road-memory (`the-remembering-roads`) and never cost a turn. This is the loop's lawful pacing valve (binding handoff L5 #4).

**The run/map is a loop with a home note, not a branch toward a boss** (`the-inked-round` / run-map-circuit). Route choice — *which pale needs to answer now, which to trust forward to ripen* — is the core strategic layer sitting above the per-turn performance. You thread the circuit an arc at a time at each waymeet, never the whole year at once. The map screen doubles as the run's autobiography (route-light brightening behind you).

## 2. The moment-to-moment loop — the performance (the working)

Traces: `audience-of-things` / **attention-engine**; `waking-of-made-things` / **waking-threshold-play**; `journey-pieces` / **journey-pieces-deck**; `gleam-runoff` / **overkill-to-standing**.

The tactical heart of the loop is **the working**: the performance you give to answer an asking. It is where cards are played and where the day's fortune is won. Pressure-first framing: *the working exists to be the place where you either overflow into Standing or fall short into dimming.* Its shape is dictated entirely by the attention engine and the waking threshold.

**The pieces on the table when a working begins:**
- **The need (the accepted asking):** a legible demand — a **waking-bar** stating how much *woken delight* the need must receive to be re-made (e.g. "the well wants its sweetness back: fill ●●●"), plus any material **micro-terms** in play (§3). The bar is the target; overfilling it is the point.
- **The room (attention):** a starting pool of **attention** provided by the **watchers present** — the woken audience-things you own (persistent artifacts, `audience-of-things`), the node's townsfolk, and the venue. Attention is a *force*, not a courtesy: you work measurably better inside more of it. This is the per-working spendable currency.
- **Your hand:** journey-pieces drawn from the pack (song, joinery, glaze, thread, dough, dance suits).

**The loop, one play at a time:**
1. **Play a piece.** It costs attention to perform. Playing it adds a link to the **work-chain**.
2. **The room leans in — the chain.** Each *uninterrupted* successive play raises the current **attention threshold** (the room gathering, `audience-of-things`: "sustained by unbroken work"). A chain is the engine's build-up; keeping it going is the skill.
3. **The waking line** (`waking-of-made-things`, exact trigger). A piece played **at or above the current attention threshold WAKES**: it takes its thin gold working-edge, pays **woken delight** into the need's waking-bar, and is **fired to your run-deck permanently** (`the-held-and-the-fired` — the fired never unfires). A piece played **below** threshold still resolves but stays **inert** — pays nothing to waking, and is paling-prone. *Things wake when watched;* spam-play in a thin room produces dull work.
4. **Stall = the chain breaks.** If you cannot or choose not to continue, the chain slackens. Attention riding it is **not lost** — it seeps back to the table and returns tomorrow (conservation). But the threshold resets, so the room must be re-gathered.
5. **The big play + the overflow.** Attention can be spent as a **multiplier** on a capstone piece. When delight **overfills** what the piece — and then the need — can hold, the **overkill converts to Standing at full rate** (`gleam-runoff`, "a full cup runs to the pourer"). This is the single most important moment in the loop: *the run's biggest Standing swings happen here, on stage, in public, at the top of the engine.* Pushing a performance past "enough" is never waste.
6. **Fulfill.** When the waking-bar fills, the need is re-made: the node **re-colors** in visible watercolor strokes, and the **glad-load** pays out (§3.3).

**The per-turn decisions that make this deep (design-for-interacting-systems, even with no L1 depth floor):**
- **Bank or spend attention** — hoard for one huge multiplier'd big play (max overkill → Standing), or spread it to guarantee the waking-bar fills (safe fulfillment, less gleam)?
- **Risk the waking line** — reach for a high-threshold play that wakes a strong piece into your run-deck, or play safe below the line and keep the chain alive?
- **Chain management** — every stall is lawful (nothing lost) but costs tempo; the tension is *reach vs. reliability.*
- **Read the room before you commit** — thin rooms (kept towns, lonely roads) cap your ceiling; thick rooms (fairs, high days, pale-town crowds) are where you gamble for the Moth-Fair overflow.

This is the loop the whole run is a frame around: **draw the morning's regard → build the room → cross the waking line → overflow into Standing or fall short → the need re-made or the light dimmed → close at the fire.**

## 3. The contract (asking) lifecycle — accept · work · fulfill · reward · escalate

Traces: `the-askings` / **escalating-contracts**; `need-takes-aim` / **two-filter-lantern-sizing**; `the-lantern-dark` / **contract-preview-ui**; `the-glad-load` / **contract-payoff-bundle**; `the-combing` / **combing-boss-tier**.

### 3.1 The two-filter lantern — how an asking is offered and accepted

Every town hangs its needs on the **asking-lantern** at the gate. What it offers you is decided by **two filters, always in this order** — this *is* the escalating-contract generator, derived lawfully in canon (`need-takes-aim`, the Merrylees hour), and it is the mechanical fulcrum of the whole loop:

- **Filter 1 — TIER, fired to peak-Standing-this-run.** The *size* of what the lantern hangs is matched to the **brightest the road has heard you be** (read from your gleam's **grain**, not a hidden counter). This reading is **fired: one-way, it never falls.** Be brilliant once and the rafters hold work sized to that brilliance forever. This is the ratchet: *the size of the asking is the compliment, and the compliment is permanent.*
- **Filter 2 — SHOWING, gated by current Standing.** Which of the sized offers are actually *lit and offerable tonight* is gated by your **present** gleam. A dimming maker watches offers **descend the ladder** — kettles, then buttons, then nothing but the rafters' dark — while overhead the great fired reading waits, unlowered, for the light to come back. **The failure spiral is telegraphed in the lantern itself,** diegetically, before any menu.

**Preview law (`the-lantern-dark` / contract-preview-ui):** exactly **one tier above tonight's showing** hangs previewed as a **warm silhouette** in the low rafters — the next size of being believed in, visible before you accept. Everything beyond that stays in the rafters' honest dark. *All* locked/out-of-tier content is warm silhouette, **never** greyed-out, padlocked, or cold black. Content-gating is worn as the world's own warm-dark, not a UI lock.

**Accept by hand (`accept-by-hand`, from `the-askings`).** You take the little crafted need-object down off its red thread. From that moment it **rides your pack** as a held contract with legible demand-iconography, and it **warms as its season shortens** — a soft, visible deadline. This is the escalating-contract *token*, physical and readable.

### 3.2 Work → the season clock on a held asking

A carried asking is worked in a performance (§2). But it is also on a clock: **carried past its season, an asking goes stale** → its need was not kept, the world's regard withdraws, and **Standing dims** (the spilling, §4). This is the lawful cost of over-committing your pack — you may hold more askings than you can answer, and the pressure is that unanswered ones sour. (Materials courted with a stated term are askings too, and stale the same way — `shy-materials` as third issuer.)

### 3.3 Fulfill → the glad-load reward

Traces: `the-glad-load` / **contract-payoff-bundle**.

Fulfilling an asking pays the **glad-load**, and its size scales directly off the **gatepost spiral** (rings-in = load-out). Crucially, one number pays across **every sub-economy at once**, so answering a need advances the whole engine:

- **bright handsels** (money — the currency layer, §5-adjacent),
- **one taught journey-piece** (deck growth — a card arriving with that town's name, `deck-autobiography`),
- **one material introduction** (a shy material now courtable this run, `two-gate-courting`),
- **map-intel chalked outward** (adjacent nodes' spirals revealed, `map-intel-preview`).

Because a skipped town's spiral compounds (§5), *the same single number raises both difficulty and reward.* This is the loop's central risk dial: answer small-and-sure now, or thread wide and let the need ripen into a poem.

### 3.4 Escalate — lawful and one-directional

Escalation has exactly two lawful sources, both already in canon, both un-declinable:

1. **Being heard of.** Answering brightly raises peak-Standing → Filter 1 fires larger → next lanterns size up. Rumor does not un-travel.
2. **The Paling's arithmetic** on anything skipped (§5): a passed need compounds its spiral, so the road brings it back *twice the work, twice the load, twice the wonder.*

**The boss tier is the combing** (`the-combing` / combing-boss-tier): when a *region's* summed spiral-count crosses threshold (seven rings = "a great asking being born"), the countryside's crossed regard **combs** the loose wash into a **great asking** — a re-making of a world-scale system (a season, a river's voice, a mountain's sleep), keyed to the brightest grain the country has heard of. Telegraphed seasons ahead by converging seams. The boss is always **weather the player routed** — the exact towns skipped, converging (§6).

## 4. Standing (gleam) — how it moves, what it gates, the fail-state

Traces: `makers-gleam` / **gleam-standing**; `gleam-runoff` / **overkill-to-standing**; `the-grain-of-gleam` / **grain-tagged-standing**; `the-spilling` / **spill-boons**; `the-two-paces-of-light` / **delayed-tellings**; `the-quiet-walk` / **quiet-walk-runend-meta**; `the-shoulder-candle` / **diegetic-standing-readout**.

Standing **is gleam** — the world's trust in you, worn as a key-light behind the shoulder. It is felt as *warmth*, not a hit-point bar. Its whole behavior derives from one mechanism (`gleam-runoff`, "a full cup runs to the pourer"): gleam is overflow that runs backward along the line of cause to the maker and clings, and it lasts exactly as long as the world holds the deed in mind.

**How it RISES:**
- **Overflow in performance (full rate, the big swings):** attention/delight beyond what a piece and its need can hold → Standing, on stage, in public (§2.5). The top of the engine is where gleam is made.
- **Glad palms (small):** doorstep askings, gifts, laughter given — kettle-scale delight in any coin the world honors (`the-doorstep-askings`).
- **Delayed tellings (`the-two-paces-of-light` / delayed-tellings):** distant delight has no live line until a **telling** lays one, one fireside a night. Inbound rises are **drawn walking the road-graph** with readable arrival dusks (pack-tilt, porch-forecast), and **land only when seen landing** — a maker brightening on a lonely road for work done three towns back. Nothing fires off-screen.

**How it FALLS — the spilling (`the-spilling` / spill-boons):**
- An **unmoved audience** (a performance that under-fills the need) or an **asking carried past its season** → regard withdraws all at once; the slackened gleam **slides off where you stand** and re-colors the current node as small ambient **boons** — worth *less* than the gleam it cost, and claimable only by **later visitors and future runs, never by the spiller.** Loss is lawful, gentle, and composts into the world. It never invites a deliberate flop (spill-boons are always EV-negative for the spiller — binding L4 handoff #1).

**What it GATES (soft benefits — "warmth, not a bar"):**
- **Which askings the lantern shows you** (Filter 2, §3.1).
- **The shy-material market** — proud stuff will not state its terms to hands the world is not currently holding in mind; the market widens as gleam rises, narrows to apprentice-tier at the bottom (`two-gate-courting`).
- **Prices** — the glad-price read (`the-glad-price`).
- **Build identity via grain (`the-grain-of-gleam` / grain-tagged-standing):** Standing is a **tagged** resource, not a bare scalar. The *kinds* of delight that earned it (bridge-grained vs. song-grained) gate *which* askings size up and *which* materials answer — giving builds identity with no class menu.

**What it NEVER interacts with:** the Paling. By the grammar of light, board-state pressure can never touch the gleam meter. Players always know which losses are the world's weather (Paling) and which are their own (spilling).

**The readout (`the-shoulder-candle` / diegetic-standing-readout):** no HUD bar. Standing is the **maker portrait's key light**, quantized into legible showing-height / market-width bands; dusk scenes are the "check your Standing" beat; grain-texture is the build-identity readout. A tally-chalk inspect overlay is available for exact steps.

**The fail-state — the Quiet Walk (`the-quiet-walk` / quiet-walk-runend-meta).** At **Standing-zero** the engine goes quiet: all but the apprentice materials refuse the hands, and the run ends into the **Quiet Walk** — a brief **playable epilogue**, the circuit walked in reverse light, no pack, no pieces, no askings, fed and warmed and asked for nothing. **This is not death — it is the world going shy.** One of the run's woken works is chosen to **persist into the meta-layer** as a permanent audience-thing / unlock / starting boon for the next maker (§5, §7). The Quiet Walk can trigger in **any season** — it keeps no calendar.

## 5. The Paling — the escalating pressure clock on the board

Traces: `the-paling` / **paling-clock**; `the-paling-arithmetic` / **paling-arithmetic-compounding**; `the-unclaimed-morning` / **dawn-income**.

The Paling is the world exhaling: it takes **knack** (competence) from places, color following, **crown-first, hearth-line last.** It is the run's escalating **pressure clock on board-state** — and, by the grammar of light, it **never touches Standing.**

**Compounding, deterministic, chalked (`the-paling-arithmetic`).** A skipped need seeps at the season's fixed rate, and **knack shores up knack**, so it compounds: keepers chalk one **spiral ring per season unanswered** on the gatepost. **Three rings is a plea, five is a poem, seven is a great asking being born.** The function is exact and legible: skip a three-ring town and you know precisely what five rings will ask *and* give back. Route strategy becomes **almanac-reading** — reading spirals is reading the future.

**The lawful reward baked into decay (`the-unclaimed-morning` / dawn-income).** Paling is not pure punishment. **Pale nodes grant richer dawns** (their local table is fuller — everything the shed stopped holding pools beneath the palest floors). So *traveling faded country carries a lawful, legible income reward* — the risk-and-reward of the map layer is physics, not a bonus table. End-of-turn surplus returns tomorrow (conservation), so the dawn economy never leaks.

**How the Paling reads on the land, always, no interface lie:** gatepost spirals ring by ring; road-marks copying a count two valleys forward; crown-grey as the map's early-warning rim; dawn-color as the honest weather-report of the table below; the reverse-vignette grey of a paling town. Difficulty lives on gateposts and hilltops, in chalk and grey and red thread — never in a menu.

**How the Paling drives the route decision:** every turn spent answering a near need is a turn the far needs ripen. The Paling is the reason the loop-with-home-note is strategically alive — the circuit's second half walks the country its first half chose to trust forward, now compounded. And it is the reason the boss exists: summed spirals crossing threshold *is* the combing (§3.4, §6).

## 6. Win condition — stand the crown, tell the year

Traces: `the-crown-of-the-year` / **crown-finale-scheduling**; `the-combing` / **combing-boss-tier**; `the-winter-telling` / **winter-telling-victory**.

**There are exactly two run-ends, and only one is bright:**
- **Standing-zero, any season → the Quiet Walk** (§4). A dimming, survivable, meta-seeding — *not* a game-over screen.
- **The calendar reaches the first still dawn.** This ends the year — but a completed year is triumphant **only if the maker is still lit AND has stood their crown.**

**The crown (`the-crown-of-the-year` / crown-finale-scheduling)** is the year's guaranteed finale, and its location and tier are **authored by the player's own route**, not imposed. The finale-scheduler computes where the year's **skip-arithmetic** — summed over inherited pre-paled ring-states — **converges**, and that crossing is where the combing combs the great asking (§3.4). Telegraphed for seasons by converging spirals and Gleaner hazel. **Every completed year gets a crown of *some* tier**, graded honestly to the year's weather:
- a **great asking** answered before the gathered bench (the country crested — the biggest audience of a walking life),
- a **five-ring poem** answered before dawn-watchers (a smaller year),
- or the **Fair's last ground of the season** (the stage the whole wheel's tellings come home to).

**Standing the crown is what gates the bright ending.** The crown is a boss-tier performance (§2 at maximum scale) — it demands the whole engine the run has built, and the ways gather around the chosen hands as legible boss-modifiers (`red-thread-days`: a raked ring raises the attention ceiling; fired staging protects board-state; courted introductions unlock proud materials).

**The victory sequence — the winter-telling (`the-winter-telling` / winter-telling-victory).** Still lit at the first still dawn with the crown stood, the maker winters at one fire as the honored teller. This is a **playable epilogue**: choose the **wintering fire** (porch / town / waymeet — each seeds a different meta-reward), then choose **the one bead that goes on walking** (the run's single persistent gift). The **year-string** — every answered asking's thread strung across the pack-flap — is the run-history UI, read bead by bead. New-game-plus is framed as **the next verse**. *You never finish a Round; you hand it on mid-song.*

## 7. How the attention engine + waking threshold drive a single turn (worked example)

Traces: `audience-of-things` / **attention-engine**; `waking-of-made-things` / **waking-threshold-play**; the whole turn assembled.

To make the loop legible end-to-end, one worked morning at a **five-ring pale town** in the Deep Gold:

1. **Dawn.** The morning issues regard. Because the node is pale (five rings), the local-table draw is **large** — a rich attention pool — plus the flat base and yesterday's returned surplus. You draw a hand of five journey-pieces. *(Trace: dawn-income, conservation.)*
2. **Read the room.** The town's crowd + two woken audience-things you own (a kettle, a last) provide a healthy starting **attention** pool. A five-ring asking's **waking-bar** is deep — this will take a real chain. *(Trace: attention-engine.)*
3. **Build the chain.** You open with two cheap joinery pieces to gather the room; each uninterrupted play raises the **waking threshold**. Your third play, a glaze piece, clears the threshold — it **wakes**, takes its gold edge, pays woken delight into the bar, and is **fired to your run-deck**. *(Trace: waking-threshold-play, the-held-and-the-fired.)*
4. **Decide: safe fill or gamble the overflow.** The bar is two-thirds full. You could play out and simply fulfill. Instead you **bank** the remaining attention and, next play, spend it as a **multiplier** on your capstone song piece.
5. **The overflow.** The capstone's delight overfills both the piece and the need. The **overkill converts to Standing at full rate** — a visible surge up the working-edge into your knuckles. Peak-Standing this run just rose → Filter 1 will fire larger at the *next* lantern. *(Trace: overkill-to-standing, two-filter-lantern-sizing.)*
6. **Fulfill.** The waking-bar fills; the well is re-made; the square re-colors in watercolor strokes; the **glad-load** pays out across money, deck, materials, and map-intel — scaled to five rings. *(Trace: contract-payoff-bundle.)*
7. **The fire.** Turn closes at a hearth; you **tell** one event from the run so far (the house wakes around it — `hearth-right`); attention surplus seeps to the table for tomorrow; a **telling of today's re-making walks the road** toward the towns ahead, and will raise their lanterns' *showing* when it lands, not before. *(Trace: delayed-tellings, courier law.)*

Every step is predictable from the three laws (§0) and the element names alone — which is exactly the L1 intuitiveness gate.
