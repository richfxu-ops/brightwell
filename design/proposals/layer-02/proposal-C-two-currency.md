# Layer 2 В· resource_types вҖ” Proposal C В· TWO-CURRENCY / SPECIALIZATION

**Proposer:** C-two-currency
**Spine:** D-001 (roguelike economy deckbuilder В· escalating contracts В· depletable Standing) В· **Aesthetic:** D-002 (wonder / whimsy / elegance)
**Seeded by:** WORLD.md L4 (Economy & Materials вҖ” "scarcity is manners, not tonnage"; the courting; handsels; the six systems) and the LOCKED GDD L1 (attention = the room; gleam = Standing; the-returning-table; handsels = currency; two-gate courting; the Paling touches board only).

**Logline:** *Four resources, each with exactly one job it alone can do; four systems that interact only because those four couple through the single act of play. Two spendable currencies (tactical attention, strategic handsels), one un-spendable gate-and-fail-resource (gleam), one un-payable board pressure (the Paling) вҖ” and a flat refusal to mint a fifth.*

---

## 0. The framing, stated as a design law

The competing instinct in an economy deckbuilder is to answer every new pressure with a new token. Roundelay's canon already forbids that instinct вҖ” **"scarcity in Roundelay is manners, not tonnage"** (L4) вҖ” and this proposal takes the instruction literally at the resource layer: **depth comes from how *few* resources couple, not from how *many* resources exist.**

So the whole game runs on **exactly two things you spend** and **two things you never spend but must read:**

| | you SPEND it | you never spend it |
|---|---|---|
| **per-turn / tactical** | **Attention** ("the room") | **The Paling** (season-seep вҖ” the weather you route, never pay) |
| **persistent / strategic** | **Handsels** ("the held" вҖ” the money) | **Standing** (gleam вҖ” the gate you keep lit, the fail-resource) |

That 2Г—2 is the entire specialization thesis. The tactical/strategic axis splits the two *spendable* currencies so they never overlap (attention cannot be banked; handsels cannot be spent inside a turn's working). The spend/never-spend axis keeps the fail-resource (Standing) and the pressure-resource (the Paling) structurally incapable of being "bought off." **Everything else in the world вҖ” materials, shavings, last-red, glimmers, introduction-jars вҖ” is a *substrate the four act on* or a *tap of one of the four*, and is deliberately NOT promoted to a currency of its own** (В§4). That refusal is the proposal.

Binding throughout: the **grammar of light** (places pale / people gleam / things wake вҖ” three channels that never cross) and the **one exchange gate** (`play-as-exchange-gate`: node-color, Standing, and card-wakefulness never convert directly вҖ” every transfer passes through a played journey-piece). And the **mint ban** (L4): *the gloaming-table mints nothing and loses nothing.* These three canon laws are what make a lean set safe (В§5).

## 1. The resource-type list

### The primary four (the lean set)

#### R1 В· **Attention** вҖ” "the room" В· TACTICAL CURRENCY (per-turn)
- **Role:** the only resource spent *inside* a worked morning; the engine fuel. It is a *budget and a momentum at once* вҖ” spent as a multiplier on a play, sustained by an unbroken work-chain, sagging on a stall.
- **Source:** the dawn вҖ” flat guaranteed base **+** local-table draw (paler node = richer dawn) **+** yesterday's node-local returned surplus. Gathered by being worth watching (woken audience-things, townsfolk, venue); never traded for.
- **Sink:** each play *splits with nothing lost* вҖ” a **SET** share works the piece (waking); the **RETURN** share seeps to *this node's* local table for tomorrow's dawn (node-bound, no caravanning). Overkill past a card's ceiling converts to Standing at full rate.
- **Trace:** `attention-engine` / `audience-of-things`; `the-gathered-room`; `dawn-income` / `the-unclaimed-morning`; `the-returning-table` / `attention-conservation`; `full-cup-overflow` / `overkill-to-standing`. L4: S1 "The Breath", S2 "The Bench Chain".
- **Grammar-of-light channel:** **the current** вҖ” canonically *none* of the three channels. L4 is explicit: *"the current cannot be tradedвҖҰ the one thing everything else is made of has no stall, no jar, and no substitute."* Attention is the conserved medium the three channels are all *made of*; it is the quiet floor. This is *why* it is the tactical currency and *why* it cannot be banked.

#### R2 В· **Handsels** вҖ” "the held" В· STRATEGIC CURRENCY (persistent, between-turns)
- **Role:** the money; the between-turns wealth layer. A handsel's worth *is how awake it is* вҖ” value in wakefulness denominations (dull / warm / singing), not coin-counts. Wealth is a juggling, never a pile: *"what stops moving, dims."*
- **Source:** whittled from apprentice-stuffs (anyone can make money) and minted from `bright-shavings` (the premium byproduct of attended work); brightened by circulation through the `glad-price`, the `glad-load`, the `waymeet-table`, and hearth-gifts.
- **Sink:** spent into delight (courting-terms met at market, buying taught pieces, `waymeet-table` swaps) вҖ” returns change a shade brighter; **or** idle-lapse: a boxed/idle handsel dims вҶ’ its light seeps to the gloaming-table вҶ’ returns as tomorrow's **dawn income** (R1). *The money's only sink is the world's only income* (mint-ban-clean, closed loop).
- **Trace:** `handsels` / `handsel-currency-decay`; `the-open-purse`; `the-glad-price` / `glad-price-read`; `bright-shavings-byproduct`; `the-waymeet-table`. L4: S3 "The Handsel Round".
- **Grammar-of-light channel:** **things wake** вҖ” the *held* sub-signature (full-surface breathing sheen, never the crisp fired line; `the-two-golds`). A handsel is a small woken *thing*; it obeys the wake channel, distinguished from a fired journey-piece only by held-sheen vs fired-edge.

#### R3 В· **Standing (gleam)** вҖ” the GATE + FAIL-RESOURCE (never spent)
- **Role:** the depletable regard that ends the run at zero (`gleam-gone-quiet` вҶ’ the Quiet Walk). Worn as a key-light behind the maker's shoulder; *warmth, not a hit-point bar.* **Gates soft benefits but is never spent** вҖ” *"a key that turns, never a coin that leaves."*
- **Source:** overflow in performance (`full-cup-overflow`, full rate, the big on-stage swings) + glad palms (doorstep askings, gifts) + delayed **tellings** walking the road-graph one fireside a night, landing only when *seen* landing (`the-walking-light`). Nothing fires off-screen.
- **Sink:** **none by spending.** It *falls* only through **the spilling** вҖ” an unmoved room or an asking carried past its season withdraws regard, which re-colors the node as `lane-glimmers` worth *less* than the gleam it cost (outcome-only loss; a deliberate flop is EV-negative). At zero вҶ’ the Quiet Walk.
- **Gates (soft):** Filter-2 lantern offers; the shy-material market width (opens the vouching); the glad-price read; and **build identity via grain** (`grain-tagged-standing` вҖ” a *tagged* resource, so *which kinds* of delight earned it decide *which* materials answer and *which* askings size up вҖ” archetype with no class menu).
- **Never interacts with:** the Paling. Board pressure can never touch the gleam meter.
- **Trace:** `gleam-standing` / `makers-gleam`; `overkill-to-standing`; `grain-tagged-standing`; `spill-boons` / `lane-glimmers`; `delayed-tellings` / `the-walking-light`; `diegetic-standing-readout` / the shoulder-candle; `quiet-walk-runend-meta`. L4: S2 (source), S4 (gate), S5 (loss).
- **Grammar-of-light channel:** **people gleam** вҖ” the maker's key-light, *only* on the maker. Quantized into legible showing-height / market-width bands; no HUD bar.

#### R4 В· **The Paling (season-seep)** вҖ” BOARD PRESSURE (never paid)
- **Role:** the run's escalation, worn as weather. Not a thing you hold вҖ” a rate that rises with the year (near-still in winter вҶ’ strongest in late summer and autumn) and compounds on anything skipped. **The escalation clock and the seasonal seep are the same curve.**
- **Source:** the seasonal seep itself (the calendar track вҖ” un-buyable, the run's master clock), advancing by *worked mornings* (dawns), never by travel legs. On the board it accretes as chalked **spiral rings** on skipped nodes (3 = plea, 5 = poem, 7 = a great asking being born).
- **Sink (how it is answered, never *paid*):** answering an asking re-makes the node вҖ” the square re-colors, the spiral resets, and `the-glad-load` pays out. You cannot spend a currency to lower it; the only lever is **play** (fulfilling the asking). Its board-side taps: richer local-table dawns over pale country (вҶ’ R1), last-red harvest in the left-behind, and the `combing` boss when a region sums to seven.
- **Trace:** `paling-clock` / `the-paling`; `paling-arithmetic-compounding` / `the-spiral-clock`; `escalation-is-the-weather`; `the-glad-load` / `contract-payoff-bundle`; `combing-boss-tier`; `crown-finale-scheduling` / `the-rising-crown`; `wander-year-run-frame`. L4: S6 "The Paling Market".
- **Grammar-of-light channel:** **places pale** вҖ” board-state only (nodes, resources, routes). *Never* touches the Standing meter, so the player always knows which losses are the world's weather.

### The two substrate resources (what the currencies act *on* вҖ” engines, not currencies)

#### R5 В· **Journey-pieces** вҖ” the deck (the engine you build)
- **Role:** the deckbuilder substrate itself вҖ” craft-fragments in craft-discipline suits (song, joinery, glaze, thread, dough, dance). Not fungible, not a currency; the *thing attention wakes and handsels/courting buy.* A woken piece fires to the run-deck permanently (`fired-vs-held`) and joins the room as audience.
- **Source (acquired, never "bought with a coin count"):** taught at fulfilled askings (`glad-load`, arriving with the town's name), gifted, traded, won, `waymeet-table` swaps, walking-with-masters.
- **Sink:** played (the exchange gate); exhausted / given away / performed to destruction; retired by last-lighting into `twice-benched` stock.
- **Trace:** `journey-pieces-deck`; `fired-vs-held`; `walking-ways-cardpools`; `deck-autobiography`.
- **Channel:** **things wake** вҖ” the *fired* sub-signature (crisp gold working-edge; `the-two-golds`).

#### R6 В· **Courted stock** вҖ” materials, ONE graded resource class (not N currencies)
- **Role:** the inputs the bench works into pieces. Held as **one resource type with a quality/temperament axis**, *not* a wallet per material вҖ” the anti-bloat move. Grades: apprentice-stuffs (floor, zero-gate) вҶ’ singing-silver, laughing-riverwood, festival-glass вҶ’ local characters (bell-bronze, hearth-slate) вҶ’ twice-benched.
- **Source (courted, never purchased):** the two-gate **courting** вҖ” first **the vouching** (a gleam gate: will the stuff state terms to hands the world holds in mind вҖ” R3's business, *no coin's*), then **the terms** (a performable micro-condition: song sung, chain unbroken, room laughing, crowd held). `introduction-jars` collapse a courtship into a gift.
- **Sink:** worked into pieces at the bench (вҶ’ R5), throwing off `bright-shavings` (вҶ’ R2); or **reverts to ordinary** if not kept warm within a season (inventory-as-garden, freshness in place of weight limits).
- **Trace:** `two-gate-courting` / `shy-materials`; `apprentice-floor-materials`; `graded-material-market`; `introduction-jars-freshness`; `the-twice-benched`. L4: S4 "The Courting Web".
- **Channel:** **the loose** (raw wash of where it lay) вҶ’ becomes **things wake** once benched. Crucially, courting is **gleam-gated but gleam is *never spent* to court** вҖ” the gate turns, no coin leaves.

## 2. The systems map вҖ” вүҘ4 interacting systems from four coupled resources

Every arrow is traceable to an L4 element fiction or to the L4 systems map (S1вҖ“S6). The claim of the framing: these systems are *distinct engines*, but they interact **densely** because the four resources couple through the one exchange gate вҖ” depth from coupling, not from token-count.

> **Substrate law (S1 В· The Breath вҖ” the master conservation loop all four drink from):**
> `gloaming-table вҶ’ dawn (R1 attention) вҶ’ the bench вҶ’ { SET into pieces (R5) | overflow to gleam (R3) | RETURN loose to the room } вҶ’ wash on places вҶ’ paling seep (R4) вҶ’ gloaming-table`. *Nothing exits it; nothing is minted (mint ban).* The four systems below are taps and tributaries of this one circulation.

### System A В· **The Bench Engine** вҖ” attention вҶ’ pieces вҶ’ gleam + handsel-fuel
`R1 attention` (+ audience) **spent through play** вҶ’ `R5 pieces` woken (deck grows, fired) вҶ’ delight given вҶ’ **overflow вҶ’ `R3 Standing`** (the big on-stage swings) **AND** attended work throws off `bright-shavings` вҶ’ **feeds `R2 handsels`**. Unspent attention **RETURNS** to the node's local table вҶ’ tomorrow's dawn (back to R1).
- *Interacts with:* B (mints handsel-fuel into it), C (consumes the materials C gates), D (its overflow raises the next lantern's tier; the glad-load refills it).
- *Trace:* L4 S2; `attention-engine`, `waking-threshold-play`, `overkill-to-standing`, `bright-shavings-byproduct`, `the-returning-table`.

### System B В· **The Handsel Round** вҖ” handsels вҮ„ courting/pieces; idle вҶ’ dawn (closed loop)
`R2 handsels` **spent** at the `glad-price` вҶ’ meet courting-terms / buy taught pieces / `waymeet-table` swaps вҶ’ build `R5`/court `R6` вҶ’ change returns brighter. Idle handsels **lapse** вҶ’ gloaming-table вҶ’ **return as `R1` dawn income**. Faucet: apprentice-stuffs + `bright-shavings` from System A.
- *Interacts with:* A (its faucet is A's byproduct; its lapse is A's fuel), C (handsels are how you *act on* the courting once gleam has vouched), D (the glad-load pays fresh bright handsels in).
- *Trace:* L4 S3; `handsel-currency-decay`, `the-glad-price`, `the-open-purse`, `the-waymeet-table`. **Closed & mint-clean:** *the money's only sink is the world's only income.*

### System C В· **The Courting Web** вҖ” gleam gates materials; materials feed the bench
`R3 Standing` (**gate, never spent**) opens **the vouching** вҶ’ `R6 courted stock` states its terms вҶ’ terms performed вҶ’ stock into the pack вҶ’ **fed to the bench (System A)** вҶ’ offcuts return as `bright-shavings` (вҶ’ System B). Apprentice-stuffs bypass the vouching (the comeback path). A courting-term accepted is an asking that can go stale вҶ’ the spilling (вҶ’ System D's compost, and an `R3` loss).
- *Interacts with:* A (supplies its inputs, receives its shavings-throw), B (handsels perform the terms), D (grain from R3 decides which materials the country answers with; glad-load delivers introduction-jars).
- *Trace:* L4 S4; `two-gate-courting`, `grain-tagged-standing`, `graded-material-market`, `introduction-jars-freshness`.

### System D В· **The Paling Market** вҖ” pressure вҶ’ bigger askings + richer dawns; answer pays every system
`R4 Paling` compounds on skipped nodes (`the-spiral-clock`) вҶ’ **bigger askings + richer local-table dawns (вҶ’ `R1`)** + last-red abundance in the left-behind. Answering an asking вҶ’ node re-colors (R4 resets) + **`the-glad-load` pays across *every* system at once:** bright handsels (вҶ’ B), one taught piece (вҶ’ A/R5), one introduction-jar (вҶ’ C/R6), map-intel chalked outward вҖ” **plus overflow gleam raises the next sizing (вҶ’ A/R3).** Seven rings вҶ’ the `combing` вҶ’ a great asking вҶ’ the largest overflow event in the game.
- *Interacts with:* **all three** вҖ” it pumps R1 (A's fuel), and one fulfillment pays B, C, *and* A simultaneously. This is the system that *couples the other three into one number* (rings-in = load-out).
- *Trace:* L4 S6; `paling-arithmetic-compounding`, `the-glad-load`, `combing-boss-tier`, `pale-route-harvest`, `crown-finale-scheduling`.

**System count claimed: 4** (A, B, C, D), each an independent engine, each pairwise-coupled to every other, all riding the S1 conservation substrate. (I deliberately claim 4, not more вҖ” the thesis is *fewest coupled resources*, and S5 "The Compost Loop" is folded into the spilling/retirement taps of C and D rather than inflated into a fifth headline system. A critic wanting a fifth can read the Compost tap as System E; the interactions only densify.)

## 3. Grammar-of-light channel per resource (+ the one exchange gate)

| Resource | Channel | Spends? | Cross-channel movement is legal *only* throughвҖҰ |
|---|---|---|---|
| R1 **Attention** | **the current** (none of the three; the medium they're made of) | yes (tactical) | a **played** piece вҖ” attention becomes fired-work (things wake) or gleam (people gleam) only via play/overkill |
| R2 **Handsels** | **things wake** (held sheen) | yes (strategic) | the **glad-price** вҖ” a performed, attended transaction; a handsel's wakefulness never silently becomes gleam or node-color |
| R3 **Standing** | **people gleam** (maker only) | **never** | **overkill** puts attentionвҶ’gleam on stage (play); gleamвҶ’board never happens (it *gates* the courting, never *pays* it) |
| R4 **The Paling** | **places pale** (board only) | **never paid** | answering an **asking** (play) re-colors the node; nothing else lowers it; it never touches the gleam meter |
| R5 **Journey-pieces** | **things wake** (fired edge) | played/exhausted | are themselves the exchange gate |
| R6 **Courted stock** | **the loose вҶ’ things wake** | courted, not spent | the **two-gate courting** (gleam vouch + performed term) |

**play-is-the-only-gate (the cross-channel law, restated for this set):** the three settlings вҖ” node-color (R4/places), Standing (R3/people), card-wakefulness (R5/things) вҖ” **never convert directly.** Attention (R1, the current) is the medium beneath all three, and the *only* operator that moves value between channels is **a played journey-piece.** Concretely, this set has **no silent conversion anywhere:**
- Handsels (things) вҶ’ materials/pieces: routed through the glad-price, a *performed* trade under attention (not a menu-buy).
- Standing (people) вҶ’ market access: a **gate that turns, never a coin that leaves** вҖ” gleam is read, never debited.
- Paling (places) вҶ’ reward: only a *fulfilled asking* (play) converts board-pressure into a glad-load.
- Attention (current) вҶ’ gleam or fired-work: only via **overkill** and **waking**, both of which *are* play.

No resource in this set can become another without passing through a card on the table. That is the deckbuilder's structural spine and it is honored exactly.

## 4. What is deliberately NOT a currency (the anti-bloat statement)

A specialization proposal is defined as much by what it refuses to mint. These are real world-elements; each is demoted to a *tap of one of the four resources*, never given its own wallet:

| Tempting "currency" | Demoted to | Why not a currency (trace) |
|---|---|---|
| **bright-shavings** | a *mint-input* feeding R2 handsels | It is *"the held home, entered early"* вҖ” a handsel already begun, not a parallel coin (`bright-shavings-byproduct`). One money, two faucets. |
| **last-red / red thread** | a *catalyst/bookmark tap* of R4 (the Paling) | Spent only on woken re-makings (slows a node's future paling) and to mark one knack for return вҖ” a board-pressure lever, not a spendable stock (`pale-route-harvest`, `red-thread-marker`). Capped: 1 node / 1 knack / 1 season. |
| **lane-glimmers** | an *ambient compost residue* (System C/D sink) | *"A sip, never a cup, and never for the spiller"* вҖ” worth well under one Standing point; a seeded pickup, not a held resource (`spill-boons`). |
| **introduction-jars** | a *gift-form of R6 courted stock* | A banked courtship, tradeable вҖ” but it *is* courted stock, not a second material economy (`introduction-jars-freshness`). |
| **materials (silver/riverwood/glass/вҖҰ)** | grades of the single **R6 courted stock** class | Held as one resource with a quality/temperament axis, not four wallets вҖ” *"scarcity is manners, not tonnage."* |
| **the calendar / crown** | the *source-rate of R4* | Un-buyable master clock; it *is* the seep curve, not a resource you accrue (`wander-year-run-frame`, `the-rising-crown`). |

The result: **two spendable currencies, one gate/fail-resource, one pressure вҖ” full stop.** No fifth token, and every world-element still has a mechanical home.

## 5. Anti-degenerate story (napkin_flags вүӨ 0)

The three canon laws вҖ” **the one exchange gate**, the **grammar of light**, and the **mint ban** вҖ” do the structural work; the lean set makes each check short.

**(a) No dominant resource.** The four are *non-substitutable by construction* вҖ” each does exactly one job no other can:
- Attention can't be banked (per-turn; RETURN is node-local, *no caravanning*; overkill diminishes past a band) вҖ” so it can never dominate the strategic layer.
- Handsels can't buy Standing (gleam comes only from overflow/tellings) and can't be spent inside a working вҖ” so money can never dominate the tactical loop or the fail-resource.
- Standing can't be spent (gates only) вҖ” so it can't be dumped for a burst; and it is *self-balancing*: the two-filter lantern makes high Standing raise the **tier/difficulty** at the same time it widens the market, so "more gleam" is never strictly better.
- The Paling can't be paid off with any currency вҖ” only answered by play.
Because no resource can do another's job, no single-resource strategy dominates; the run *requires* running all four systems.

**(b) On-curve clearable.** Difficulty and reward ride the **same number**: the gatepost spiral (rings-in = load-out). The weather floor + calendar-floored crown raise the *minimum* need with the season (`escalation-is-the-weather`, `the-rising-crown`), and the bench engine's peak-Standing (вҶ’ bigger glad-loads, wider market, easier wakings) scales with *engagement*. A competently reaching maker's output climbs with the weather; a static engine is *out-grown* on schedule (L1 В§5). Clearable, and clearable *only by growing* вҖ” which closes the turtle dodge via the win-gate, not via decay.

**(c) No degenerate loop.** Each known napkin obligation (L4 "Handed to game-architect", В§716вҖ“719, plus the glad-price bound В§606) is discharged by the lean set:
