  starts pre-paled (+1/+2/+3 rings per depth); one dial raises askings, dawns, harvests, and
  combing pace together.
- **G5 — the philosophy underneath.** Leaning: thin-power meta — no permanent stat inheritance;
  the meta carries meaning (beads, names, unlocked temperaments) and opt-in depth (G4), never
  strength.
