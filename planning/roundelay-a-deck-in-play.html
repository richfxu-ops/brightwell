Step one
The deck — 12 cards

Every run opens with 7 plain apprentice cards everyone shares, plus your Way's 5 signature cards. The Kilnfast's five are all joinery (woodwork) — because its trick is stacking one craft so every piece feeds the next.

Play The Fired Beam — the payoff

Pour the rest of the room (3) onto it, multiplied by your 2-link chain (+2) = +5. The Beam now holds 9.

→ 9 is past its wake-mark of 7, so it WAKES ✦ — fired into your deck forever, and it seats as your 5th joinery in the crowd.
→ 9 is 1 past its ceiling of 8, so that 1 overflows to Standing.
→ On waking it fills the need by your joinery count (5) — the need was 4, so it's cleared with 1 to spare. The town blooms back into colour and pays you a taught card, bright coins, and a map hint.

Why it wins
The same deck, getting stronger every day

The Kilnfast barely changes its cards — it just accumulates fired joinery, and each one makes the room a little bigger. Early on you can only wake small pieces; by autumn your ordinary morning wakes the capstone. That rising floor is the whole strategy.
